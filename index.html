Why Choose Us

Every business operates on a foundation of technology. But not every business is a technology company. WonderOps is a technology partner helping others build awesome technical teams.

What we really want you to know about us is what makes us different. We believe at the core of our company that diversity on technical teams results in better outcomes. Even though the IT industry in the United States continues to grow, women's participation is shrinking. We think this is a problem. It weakens the technology ecosystem and prevents most companies from achieving optimal results. Our goals are centered around helping companies maximize the benefits attained through building gender diverse technical teams.

At the heart of our company is our mission to be a destination technology employer for women in IT and a place for companies to turn to for top female technology talent. Studies have shown diversity in the workplace to directly impact results like financial outcomes, higher productivity and greater team collaboration. Companies seeking diversity in the workplace are winning in their markets!
