Why Choose Us

DevOps has many definitions. We prefer the following definition that best describes our company service objectives.

"DevOps is the practice of operations and development engineers participating together in the entire service lifecycle, from design through the development process to production support."



DevOps is also characterized by operations staff making use many of the same techniques as developers for their systems work. That's where we come in. We help companies implement DevOps from the infrastructure perspective, enabling the management of infrastructure as code and supporting Continuous Delivery of software projects.
